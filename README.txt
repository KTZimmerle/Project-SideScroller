How to play:

1. Unzip Zector Wars - Demo.zip
2. Open Zector Wars - Demo folder
3. Double click Zector Wars.exe
4. Instructions to play are as follows:
	w/a/s/d or up/down/left/right to move ship
	collect powerups to access ship's upgrades
	collect successive powerups to access more options
	press numpad enter or C to activate ship upgrade
	numpad 0 or Z to fire main weapon (changes between alt fire and laser upgrades)
	numpad . or X to fire missile weapon (requires missile upgrade)
	score as high as possible before you run out of lives. Enjoy!
